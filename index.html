About V.I.G.I.L.

V.I.G.I.L. is an independent operational body established to protect, oversee, and advance the fundamental rights of persons deprived of their liberty. Acting in accordance with United Nations principles, we ensure that human dignity is maintained even within the most restrictive institutional frameworks.
